What makes Radiant AI different from other AI or generic reporting tools ? 

We're focused on enhancing productivity for ESG teams. Unlike other AI solutions we're focused on extracting ESG relevant data from documents. Making our tools more effective for ESG tasks than stand alone generic chat solutions like ChatGPT or Claude.

Leveraging state of the art retrieval and generation mechanisms we're able to help you speed up assessments, analysis and research. We are not a reporting tool. Instead we focus on speeding up assessments, due diligence checks and analysis.  By helping ESG teams speed up their work, we're enabling them to focus on higher impact and more strategic activities.

Are my documents safe ? 

We strictly adhere to data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act of 2018. Our operations are tailored to comply with any other applicable data protection laws in the jurisdictions we serve. We are committed to achieving the ISO 27001 certification, a globally recognised standard for information security management systems (ISMS), reflecting our commitment to the highest standards of data security.

What exactly is the Analyse feature ? 

Our Analyse feature lets you chat with any ESG or sustainability reports. Get instant answers to your most pressing questions. Quickly extract data and key information from multiple documents at any given time. It can also help summarise regulations, help you draft reports and much more.

What exactly is the Assesssment feature? 

Our Assessment product is designed to help you scan through dozens of documents and lets you auto-populate ESG answers in seconds. Now you can carry our various ESG compliance checks in a matter of minutes and not days or weeks.

What are the benefits of using Radiant AI?

Radiant AI empowers ESG consulting teams to get more done faster. We can help you efficiently scale your operations by allowing you to take on more client work without compromising quality. We help reduce burnout by making it more efficient for teams to do their day to day jobs especially the parts they may not enjoy.
